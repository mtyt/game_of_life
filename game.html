Instructions

Draw live cells by clicking. Hit Play or press space-bar to start. Drawing new cells will automatically pause.
Game of Life rules are simple:
  • Live cells with 2 or 3 neighbours survive
  • Dead cells with 3 live neighbours become alive
  • Neighbours are counted as 8 immediately surrounding cells
On this page, you can change the speed using the slider. 1 means 1 update per second. Very high speeds are limited by your computer's number-crunching powers (and admittably, my implementation). The total size of the grid can be changed, as well as the number of grid cells. Use the Random Seed input to generate a random starting pattern with a certain percentage of living cells. The Presets menu can be used to load pre-defined starting patterns.
